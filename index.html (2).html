No. Pawket provides membership discounts, prepaid vouchers, and virtual vet consults. There are no claims or reimbursements.
How do vouchers work?
You get a digital code to redeem at participating clinics for listed services. Clinics invoice Pawket directly. Simple.

Yes. If you redeem a prepaid voucher and cancel within 90 days of signup, the retail value of the redeemed service will be billed to your payment method.
